Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01-09-2025

1. Information We Collect

No Personal Information from Children. We do not knowingly collect personal information (name, email, phone number, address, or precise location) from children under 13.

Anonymous Non-Personal Data: We may collect technical data such as device model, app version, and anonymous usage statistics to improve app quality. This data cannot identify a child.

2. How We Use Information

  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Enhance the in-app experience and artwork

3. Advertising & Purchases

Color Fun Kids is designed for children. If ads are shown, they will be:

  • From Google Play certified ad networks only
  • Family-friendly and non-personalized
  • Clearly labeled and not misleading

Any in-app purchases will require parental confirmation and will not collect PII from children.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information about children. We may share anonymous aggregated data with vendors for analytics and app performance support.

5. Children's Privacy

We comply with COPPA and the Google Play Families Policy. If you believe we have collected personal data from your child, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.
